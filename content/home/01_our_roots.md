## Our Roots
Ascend Next Level became a Community Interest Company in November 2024. A decade ago we recognised young people's mental health was in decline: there was increased isolation due to the attraction of screens, and a break from spiritual roots and a relationship with Jesus. We began to dream with God as to how things could be different for young people and lead them to have a better **relational life quality**. The Lord gave us a vision to resource and gather young people through music, creativity, sports and adventure and a place to explore spirituality where they may encounter Him at a deep level.
Since then, we have been praying for young people and, as a result, ANL has been born and slowly we have been connecting with them!
