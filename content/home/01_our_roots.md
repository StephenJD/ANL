Ascend Next Level became a Community Interest Company on 24.11.25 and are slowly connecting with young people, so far in Chinley and Whaley Bridge. A decade ago we recognised young peoples' mental health was worsening, their screen use was increasing and their interest in Jesus was declining. The Lord gave us a vision of young people gathering for sports, music and a place to encounter Him deeply. Since then we've been praying for young people and as a result, Ascend Next Level has been born!

**Currently Active In:** Chinley & Whaley Bridge
